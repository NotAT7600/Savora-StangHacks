# PantryAI - Quick Start Guide

## What is PantryAI?
PantryAI is an AI-powered food waste reduction platform that helps you transform leftover ingredients into delicious recipes using ChatGPT.

## Features
✅ **ChatGPT Recipe Generation** - Real recipes generated by OpenAI's ChatGPT (gpt-4o-mini)
✅ **Sustainability Tracking** - Track your environmental impact and cost savings
✅ **Community Feed** - Share and discover recipes from other users
✅ **Impact Dashboard** - Visualize your food waste reduction journey
✅ **Save Recipes** - Bookmark your favorite recipes for later

## Getting Started

### 1. Sign Up
- Navigate to the Login page
- Click "Sign Up" tab
- Enter your email and password
- You'll be automatically logged in

### 2. Generate Your First Recipe
- Go to "Generate Recipe" from the navigation
- Add your leftover ingredients (e.g., "chicken", "rice", "broccoli")
- Click "Generate Recipe with AI"
- Wait 2-5 seconds for ChatGPT to create your recipe
- View your personalized recipe with ingredients and instructions

### 3. Explore Community Recipes
- Visit "Community Feed" to see recipes from other users
- Click on any recipe to view full details
- Save recipes you like by clicking the bookmark icon
- Infinite scroll to discover more recipes

### 4. Track Your Impact
- Check "My Impact" to see your sustainability statistics
- View charts showing your recipe generation trends
- See your total money saved and food waste prevented
- Track your environmental contribution

### 5. Dashboard Overview
- Visit "Dashboard" for a quick overview
- See your saved recipes
- View impact statistics at a glance
- Quick access to generate new recipes

## How Recipe Generation Works

### Input
Add ingredients you have available using the tag-based input system:
- Type an ingredient name
- Press Enter or click the + button
- Remove ingredients by clicking the X on each tag

### AI Processing
When you click "Generate Recipe with AI":
1. Your ingredients are sent to ChatGPT via secure Edge Function
2. ChatGPT (gpt-4o-mini model) creates a custom recipe
3. Recipe is saved to your account
4. Your impact statistics are automatically updated

### Output
You'll receive a complete recipe with:
- Creative recipe name
- Full ingredients list with quantities
- Step-by-step cooking instructions
- Estimated cooking time
- Estimated cost savings

## Important Notes

### Real AI Responses
- All recipes come directly from ChatGPT API
- No fake or placeholder data is ever generated
- If the API fails, you'll see an error message
- Simply try again if you encounter an error

### Privacy & Security
- Your API calls are processed server-side
- OpenAI API key is securely stored
- Your recipes are private to your account
- You control what recipes to share in the community feed

### First User Privilege
- The first user to sign up automatically becomes an admin
- Admin users have full access to all features
- Regular users can create, save, and share recipes

## Tips for Best Results

### Ingredient Selection
- Add 3-7 ingredients for best results
- Include a protein, carb, and vegetable when possible
- Be specific (e.g., "chicken breast" vs "chicken")
- Common pantry items work great (rice, pasta, eggs, etc.)

### Recipe Variety
- Try different ingredient combinations
- ChatGPT creates unique recipes each time
- Same ingredients can produce different recipes
- Experiment with cuisines by adding specific spices

### Sustainability Impact
- Each recipe you generate counts toward your impact stats
- Track your progress over time
- See how much money and food you're saving
- Share your achievements with the community

## Troubleshooting

### Recipe Generation Fails
- Check your internet connection
- Verify you've added at least one ingredient
- Try again (API may be temporarily busy)
- Check console for detailed error messages

### Can't See Recipes
- Make sure you're logged in
- Check that recipes were successfully generated
- Refresh the page if needed

### Saved Recipes Not Showing
- Verify you clicked the bookmark icon
- Check your Dashboard for saved recipes
- Ensure you're logged into the correct account

## Technical Details

### Technology Stack
- **Frontend**: React + TypeScript + Tailwind CSS + shadcn/ui
- **Backend**: Supabase (PostgreSQL + Edge Functions)
- **AI**: OpenAI ChatGPT (gpt-4o-mini)
- **Charts**: Recharts
- **Animations**: Framer Motion

### API Information
- **Model**: gpt-4o-mini
- **Average Response Time**: 2-5 seconds
- **Cost per Recipe**: ~$0.0003 (very affordable)
- **Rate Limits**: Based on OpenAI account tier
